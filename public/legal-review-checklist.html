ColleagueAI Legal Review Checklist

Internal review page. This checklist is for operational legal review before paid launch. It is not legal advice and should be reviewed by qualified counsel.
Current legal launch status: Paid commercial launch remains blocked until Terms, Privacy Policy, Licence Agreement, Partner Agreement, refund/withdrawal wording, and Stripe checkout wording are legally approved.

1. Documents to Review

  • Terms of Service
  • Privacy Policy
  • Licence Agreement
  • Partner / Affiliate Agreement
  • Cookie / tracking notice
  • Checkout and refund wording
  • Download / entitlement wording
  • AI-agent disclaimer and risk positioning
  • Partner fraud and commission rules

2. Terms of Service

  • Confirm legal entity name, registered address, and contact email.
  • Confirm governing law and jurisdiction.
  • Clarify whether customers are B2B, B2C, or both.
  • Define exactly what the customer receives after purchase.
  • Define one-time access, subscription, lifetime access, revocation, or time limitation.
  • Confirm refund, cancellation, and withdrawal wording.
  • Confirm limitation of liability and no guaranteed ROI/compliance result.
  • Remove all draft or placeholder wording before launch.

3. Privacy Policy

  • Identify the data controller.
  • List personal data collected: name, email, payment metadata, partner code, logs, analytics, support messages, demo-agent usage.
  • Identify legal bases for processing.
  • Identify processors: Vercel, Stripe, KV/Upstash, Cloudflare R2, Sentry, analytics provider, email provider if used.
  • Confirm retention periods, user rights, international transfers, and security measures.
  • Confirm AI-demo data handling and whether prompts are stored or sent to third-party AI providers.
  • Confirm Stripe payment wording does not imply ColleagueAI stores full card data.

4. Licence Agreement

  • Define licence type: non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable or perpetual.
  • Define allowed use: internal business use, evaluation, client work, modification, redistribution.
  • Define prohibited use: resale, public redistribution, model training, reverse engineering, unlawful use.
  • Define ownership of ColleagueAI materials, customer data, and outputs.
  • Define update and support obligations, if any.
  • Confirm termination and download-entitlement rules.

5. Partner / Affiliate Agreement

  • Define partner eligibility and registration process.
  • Define commission rate, calculation base, payout timing, and clawback rules.
  • Confirm commission applies once per one-time purchase unless otherwise agreed.
  • Define fraud controls: self-referral, fake leads, coupon abuse, prohibited advertising, malformed partner codes.
  • Define termination, withholding, tax responsibility, and affiliate-disclosure obligations.

6. AI-Specific Review

  • Confirm AI agents are positioned as decision-support tools, not final decision-makers.
  • Confirm no claim of legal, medical, tax, investment, or regulated professional advice.
  • Confirm human review is required for high-impact outputs.
  • Confirm CAI Score does not imply official certification.
  • Confirm production-grade, safety, compliance, and ROI claims are supportable.
  • Confirm no claim that the system guarantees compliance with the EU AI Act, GDPR, financial regulation, or employment law.

7. Digital-Commerce Review

  • Confirm price display, VAT/tax handling, and payment terms.
  • Confirm refund and withdrawal wording before checkout.
  • Confirm immediate digital delivery consent where required.
  • Confirm receipt/invoice process.
  • Confirm support route for payment and download issues.

8. Data Protection and Security

  • Confirm no secrets are exposed in frontend code.
  • Confirm production secrets are stored only in Vercel, Stripe, Cloudflare, and Upstash dashboards.
  • Confirm signed download links are time-limited.
  • Confirm webhook verifies Stripe signatures.
  • Confirm entitlement and partner stores are protected.
  • Confirm logs do not expose secrets, card data, or sensitive customer inputs.

9. Stripe Launch Blocker

  • Add STRIPE_SECRET_KEY in Vercel Production.
  • Add STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET in Vercel Production.
  • Configure webhook endpoint: https://www.colleagueai.ai/api/webhook.
  • Subscribe to checkout.session.completed.
  • Configure Payment Link metadata: agent_slug = all.
  • Complete test checkout.
  • Confirm webhook creates entitlement.
  • Confirm signed download link works for entitled customer.
  • Confirm unauthorized users cannot access artifacts.
