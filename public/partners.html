Questions

Common questions, direct answers.

How does attribution work?

When someone clicks your partner link, your code is saved in their browser for 30 days. If they buy any agent during that window — even days later, even if they return directly — the sale is credited to you. Attribution passes through our Stripe checkout metadata automatically.

When and how do I get paid?

Commissions are tallied monthly. Once you have accumulated sales, we contact you at your registered email to arrange a bank transfer or other payment method. There is no minimum threshold — your first sale pays out.

Can I resell agents under my own brand?

Yes. You can refer clients passively (they click your link and buy directly) or buy the agent licence yourself and deploy it for a client under a service agreement. Both routes earn you credit. Contact us at hello@colleagueai.ai to discuss reseller terms.

What if my client has already seen Colleague AI?

As long as they click your partner link before purchasing, you are credited. If they have already completed a purchase without your link, that sale cannot be attributed retroactively.

Is there a cap on how much I can earn?

No cap. Refer one client or a hundred. Commission applies to every sale attributed to your code, at the same rate, with no volume tiers that reduce it.

What if I lose my partner link?

Your code is derived deterministically from your email address. Come back to this page, enter the same name and email, and you get the exact same code and link every time.

Is there a formal partner agreement?

By generating a link you accept that commissions are paid at the rate shown at the time of sale, apply only to completed and non-refunded purchases, and are non-transferable. A full written agreement is available on request at hello@colleagueai.ai.
