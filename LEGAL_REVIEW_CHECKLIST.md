# Legal Review Checklist

## Pages requiring final approval

- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Licence Agreement
- Partner Agreement
- Refund Policy
- Cookie Policy, if applicable
- AI acceptable-use wording
- Liability limitation
- Warranty disclaimer
- Jurisdiction and governing law
- Data processing wording
- Subprocessor disclosure
- Partner payout and clawback rules

## Must remove before production monetization

- "Draft"
- "Pending legal review"
- Placeholder company details
- Placeholder email addresses
- Placeholder jurisdiction
- Placeholder refund terms

## Commercial policies to confirm

- Is the product sold as software, consulting, template, agent package, or SaaS?
- Is VAT collected?
- Are refunds allowed?
- What happens after chargeback?
- Is partner commission clawed back after refund?
- Are partners required to submit tax details?
- Are self-referrals prohibited?
