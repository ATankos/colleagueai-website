The CAI Score framework

Every agent in the Colleague AI catalogue is evaluated against the CAI Score, a five-tier risk classification framework that defines what an agent is and is not permitted to do. Tier L1 covers read-only informational agents. Tier L5 covers agents that act with full autonomy in well-defined, bounded domains. The tier level is displayed on each agent's product page alongside the specific guardrails that enforce it. Because the tier definitions are part of the product itself, your legal and compliance teams can review exactly what an agent does — before procurement.

Deployment and data handling

Colleague AI agents are designed to run in your environment, not ours. The demo uses a sandboxed cloud instance with synthetic data. In production, agents are deployed into your VPC or on-premise infrastructure so that no customer data ever leaves your perimeter. There are no third-party AI model calls to external APIs in the data path: all inference runs inside your environment.
